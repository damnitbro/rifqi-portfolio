# Selected Project Directions

## 1. Ministry of Health Campaign Design

**Best purpose:** Professional credibility and campaign-system thinking.

Show:

- The communication problem
- Mandatory logos and copy
- Layout exploration
- Visual hierarchy
- Static and motion adaptations
- Exact personal contribution
- Verified deliverable count

Avoid displaying a long wall of similar posts.

## 2. SOCA 3D Visual Development

**Best purpose:** Demonstrate 3D production and reusable visual assets.

Show:

- References
- Character or model development
- Silhouette and material tests
- Lighting setup
- Campaign applications
- Reusable camera, lighting, or material system
- Credits for modelling, rigging, and external assets

## 3. Real-Estate Content System

**Best purpose:** Demonstrate repeatable video and motion production.

Show:

- Footage organization
- Story structure
- Reusable title and information cards
- Long-form and short-form adaptations
- Before-and-after editing examples
- Number of videos delivered
- Defensible channel or engagement data

## 4. Personal 3D Project

**Best purpose:** Demonstrate your personal visual standard.

Show:

- One strong concept
- References and blockout
- Materials
- Lighting iterations
- Final render
- Detail crops
- Clay or wireframe view
- Honest reflection

Remove basic exercises that are weaker than your professional work.
