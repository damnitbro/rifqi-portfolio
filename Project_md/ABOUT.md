# About

I am Rifqi Adiantama, a Jakarta-based visual designer specializing in 3D, motion, and digital campaign production.

My experience covers institutional communication, social-media campaigns, real-estate marketing, video editing, and visual development. I am especially interested in projects that require one idea to remain clear and consistent across static graphics, motion content, video, and 3D applications.

Rather than treating each deliverable as an isolated design, I aim to build flexible visual systems that can be adapted for different platforms, audiences, and formats.

## Capabilities

- Campaign design
- Social-media content systems
- 3D visualization
- Motion graphics
- Video editing
- Art direction
- Visual identity application
- Presentation design

## Working Process

### Understand

Identify the audience, objective, required information, platform, and practical limitations.

### Define

Establish the hierarchy, composition, typography, imagery, color, and motion principles.

### Produce

Build reusable assets that remain consistent across different dimensions and formats.

### Refine

Revise the work based on feedback while protecting clarity, accessibility, and visual hierarchy.
